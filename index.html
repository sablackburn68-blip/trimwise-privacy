Privacy Policy for TrimWise

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

TrimWise (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the TrimWise app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information, such as your email address and login credentials
  • Profile information, such as age, sex, height, weight, activity level, and goals
  • Nutrition and fitness data, including food logs, weight logs, and goal settings
  • Camera or barcode scan input used for food scanning and meal analysis
  • Subscription and purchase status information provided by Apple and RevenueCat
  • Basic device and app usage information to improve app performance and reliability

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition, weight, and fitness features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may use trusted third-party service providers to operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, authentication, and subscription management providers.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You may be able to:

  • Update your profile and goals in the app

  • Manage subscriptions through your Apple account settings

6. Subscriptions

TrimWise may offer optional paid subscriptions. Billing, renewal, and cancellation are handled by Apple through your App Store account and are subject to Apple’s terms and conditions.

7. Children’s Privacy

TrimWise is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
